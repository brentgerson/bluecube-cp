What Is River Mode?

Most cold plunges are still water. You sit in them and a warm pocket forms around your body, reducing the effectiveness of the cold exposure.

River Mode is our proprietary hydro-agitation system. It circulates water around your entire body — like standing in a cold river. The thermal barrier never forms. You get true, even cold exposure from the first second to the last.

No other manufacturer offers this. It's the single biggest reason our customers say BlueCube feels different from everything else they've tried.
